The Andes

Part of the largest mountain range in the wold

The Ecuadorian Andes, also known as "La Sierra” and the “Highlands”, is probably the most visited region of the country because of its weather (colder than the one you find in the coast and amazaon regions) and the cultural and natural diversity offers to its visitors
This region has the most developed tourist industry in the country, providing tourists and locals with a wide range of accommodation, world-class eateries, shopping centers and many cultural festivals. In addition to this, most of these facilities are easily accessible from the Ecuadorian capital of Quito (in the map), divided by the ecuadorian line and were you can visit the "Centro del mundo" monumnet.
Hiking, mountain biking, kayaking, swimming are only a few recreational activities that the Ecuadorian Andes will be able to offer you. But if you prefer, you can join a relaxing break into the thermal springs, exhaust yourself on a week long hike or just lie in your five-star hotel room, the Sierra has it all.
