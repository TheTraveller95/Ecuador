Who am I and why a page about Ecuador?

I am a 24 years old guy who has always been attracted by the tech world. I am Italian, but moved to Ireland 2 years ago from Ecuador, where I lived for almost 3 years. That experience really changed my life. I learnt to see the world in a different way. I met incredible people, always friendly, happy and that are always ready to help you in every situation. I ate some of the most delicious plates I have ever tried, so different depending on the region they are from. Just to mention some examples, in the coast you can do breakfast with encebollado, bolones or ceviche, in the Andes you have to try the humita and the hornado and in the Amazon cities you will try chicha de yuca y chonta and the Pinchos Mayones. During my stay there I also visited many amazing place, saw incredible landscapes and walked through the wild nature. And again, every region has something you cannot miss if go there: the amazing sunsets in the coast and Galapagos, the old inca villages (like Ingapirca) in the Andes and all the animal species in the Amazon Rainforest. So, I think that now is quite ovious why I chose Ecuador as the topic of my project. I wanted to show you a place in the earth that I really love, trough a tool (coding) that I really enjoy using.
